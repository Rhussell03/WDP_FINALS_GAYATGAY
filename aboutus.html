About Kuya Rhussell

When Eating Turns to Cooking

I am Rhussell a I.T student in Adamson University, the eldest son in my family (they often called me Kuya so what's where I got the idea), and has burning passion for eating and cooking!

From experimenting with international dishes to creating Filipino comfort food, I bring personality, comedy, and flavor to every plate I make. I aim to share this passion of mine to others, share my discoveries in the culinary world, my own twist and recepies. That's why join me, Kuya Rhussell in my delicious, flavourful(or maybe tasteless, I don't know), and experimental culinary journey!!
